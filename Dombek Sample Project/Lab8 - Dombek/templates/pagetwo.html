Biomedical Metrology

Definition

Traceable measurements and mathematical models in the field of medical physics and biomedical sciences by ensuring bioinstrumentation is valid for use.

Some test equipment include:

  1. Safety Analyzers
  2. Patient Simulators
  3. Defibrillator Analyzers
  4. Pulse Oximetry Testers
  5. Electro-Surgical Generator Analyzers
